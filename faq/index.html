Everything you might want to know before you buy.

How do I get my printable after I buy?

Right after checkout you get an instant download link, and Payhip also emails it to you. Your files are yours forever, download them on any device.

Do I need a printer?

Most pages are made to print at home on regular paper, but you can also use them on a tablet or phone, or print them at any print shop. Nothing fancy needed.

Are the printables in English and Spanish?

Yes! Most products include both an English and a Spanish PDF, plus a short "Start Here" page so you always know which file to open.

What ages are these for?

The kids' activities are designed for ages 4 to 10, and the planners and safety guides are for the grown-ups. They're made for families who are new to the outdoors, no experience needed.

What is your refund policy?

Because these are instant digital downloads they can't be returned. But if something's wrong with your file, email adventurerpaola@gmail.com and I'll make it right.
