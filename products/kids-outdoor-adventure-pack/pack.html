Hey, adventure parent! 👋

Para ti, mamá o papá aventurero

I made this so getting the kids outside is EASY: no planning, no screens, no "I'm bored." Print the pages, grab a pencil and a clipboard, and let them explore. Works in a backyard, a park, or a campsite!

Lo hice para que sacar a los niños al aire libre sea fácil: sin pantallas y sin aburrimiento. ¡Imprime, dale un lápiz y a explorar!

How to use it · Cómo usarlo
🗎️ Print the pages you like (one per kid so nobody fights!).
📎 Clip them to a clipboard or fold in a pocket.
✏️ Hand over a pencil and GO. Give the certificate at the end!
Safety first · Seguridad primero: Kids should stay where a grown-up can see them, never taste or touch unknown plants, mushrooms or animals, and wash hands after. In bear country, keep food packed away. Adult supervision required at all times. Se requiere supervisión de un adulto en todo momento.

Be a Safety Captain! · ¡Sé el capitán!

My Campsite Rules ⛰️

Mis reglas: read them out loud, then you're in charge!
  • 1Stay where a grown-up can see me.Me quedo donde un adulto me vea.👀
  • 2Food stays packed away: never in the tent!La comida siempre guardada.🍱
  • 3See a big animal? Stay calm, don't run, walk back slowly.Ante un animal grande: tranquilo, regreso despacio.🐻
  • 4Never touch or taste plants, berries or mushrooms.Nunca toco ni pruebo plantas ni hongos.🍄
  • 5Stay on the trail and wear my shoes.Me quedo en el sendero y uso zapatos.👞
  • 6Wash my hands before I eat.Me lavo las manos antes de comer.🥣
