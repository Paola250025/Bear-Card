Hey, fellow adventure parent

Para ti, mamá o papá aventurero

I made this so getting the kids outside feels easy — no planning, no screens, no "I'm bored." Print the pages you want, grab a pencil and a clipboard, and let them explore. Every activity works in a backyard, a park, or a campsite.

Lo hice para que sacar a los niños al aire libre sea fácil — sin pantallas y sin aburrimiento. Imprime las páginas que quieras y deja que exploren.

How to use it · Cómo usarlo
1. Print the pages you like (print more than one so siblings each get their own).
2. Clip them to a clipboard or fold into a pocket.
3. Hand over a pencil and go. Award the certificate at the end!
Safety first · Seguridad primero: Kids should always stay where a grown-up can see them, never taste or touch unknown plants, mushrooms or animals, and wash hands after exploring. In bear country, keep food packed away and stay close together. Adult supervision required at all times.
Los niños deben permanecer siempre a la vista de un adulto y nunca tocar ni probar plantas, hongos o animales desconocidos. Se requiere supervisión de un adulto en todo momento.

Activity 3

My Campsite Safety Rules

Mis reglas de seguridad — read them out loud, then be the safety captain!
  • Stay where a grown-up can see me.Me quedo donde un adulto me vea.
  • Food stays packed away — never in the tent.La comida siempre guardada, nunca en la tienda.
  • If I see a big animal, I stay calm, don't run, and walk back slowly to a grown-up.Si veo un animal grande, me quedo tranquilo y regreso despacio con un adulto.
  • I never touch or taste plants, berries or mushrooms.Nunca toco ni pruebo plantas ni hongos.
  • I stay on the trail and wear my shoes.Me quedo en el sendero y uso mis zapatos.
  • I wash my hands before I eat.Me lavo las manos antes de comer.
